Executive Masterâ€™s Programs

For students who have already completed a U.S. masterâ€™s degree

New England College’s executive programs are designed for working professionals interested in advancing their qualifications in their chosen career fields. The executive programs provide students with insightful knowledge while gaining real-world experience and hands-on learning outcomes through the required internship or job.*

*Eligible F-1 students will complete their required internship or job through CPT (Curricular Practical Training) authorization. This is an integral part of the Executive program curriculum. Internships or jobs must directly relate to the objectives of the studentâ€™s chosen program of study. One year of full-time CPT eliminates a studentâ€™s eligibility for OPT at the degree level.

Programs
These programs are delivered in a hybrid format in which students complete the program in five or six terms, with online work during the semester and a three-day on-campus residency each semester. Students will be required to be on campus in Henniker, New Hampshire, three times a year to complete the residencies. Residencies are held on weekends to accommodate the required internship schedule.
