Admissions

A Transpersonal Education University

Starting your academic journey is a big step

Our dedicated Admissions team is here to guide you through the admissions process and identify the right program for you and your career goals. To speak with our Admissions team, please use the following methods:

  • Call 888-820-1484 (and press 1)
  • Send an email to: admissions@sofia.edu
  • Fill out a Request More Information form to receive our emails and get contacted by an
    Admissions Counselor

Now accepting applications for Spring 2024!

Sofia University offers flexible, affordable, and accredited education at the bachelors, masters, and doctoral levels with a curriculum based on the intellectual, emotional, spiritual, physical, social, and creative aspects of life.

Our top priority is ensuring that our students receive a solid educational experience that allows them the opportunity to balance their academic journey along with their personal life journey.

Start your educational journey at our Palo Alto or Costa Mesa campus today!
