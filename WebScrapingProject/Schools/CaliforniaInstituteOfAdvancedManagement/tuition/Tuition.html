Cost & Aid

Our programs blend knowledge and practice to prepare you for success in any career environment and are a long-term investment in your future.

We understand that graduate school is an investment. Our team is available to answer your questions and help you determine ways to finance your education. Please contact the Financial Aid Office at (626) 350-1500 or [email protected] for assistance.

Cost & Aid FAQs

  • Do you offer information sessions?

    Yes. We regularly offer sessions and prospective student events throughout the year. In addition, you can contact the Office of Admissions at [email protected] to schedule a personal visit.

  • How do I apply?

    At California Institute of Advanced Management, it's easy to enroll in our programs. Start with completing the online application and our experienced support staff will walk you step-by-step to complete the next steps until you are ready for the start.


    Click here to begin your application.

  • Do you allow students to attend part-time?

    Yes, domestic students have the option to enroll as full-time or part-time students. Full-time enrollment typically constitutes a course load of 6 or more units in a term (2 courses). International students are required to maintain full-time enrollment throughout the duration of their degree program.

  • When can I apply?

    CiAM offers rolling admissions so there’s always a future term available if you meet the application deadline. Please contact the Office of Admissions at 626.350.1500 or [email protected] for detailed instructions on how to apply.

  • How long does it take to hear back about acceptance to the program?

    We usually respond immediately, but we will appreciate if you email [email protected] to update us about your progress. You are always welcome to call 626.350.1500 or email us to speak with our dedicated Admissions Specialists.

  • My work experience is less than a year. Can I still apply to CIAM?

    If your work experience is less than a year, please submit an admission petition to the Dean’s Office to request a waiver. The Academic Council may be involved for evaluation and recommendation, please contact Office of Admissions at [email protected] or call 1(626)-350-1500 for more information.

  • Do I need to take the GRE/GMAT?

    No, candidates for admission are evaluated holistically based on their merits and potential to succeed at CiAM. For admissions decisions, we use information from academic performance, work experience, leadership positions, extracurricular interests, unique talents, and the personal interview. We consider the applicant as a whole individual, not select parts such as GPA or GRE/GMAT test scores. Efforts are made to match the candidate’s expectation with that of the CiAM program design. Please contact Office of Admissions at [email protected] or call 1-626-350-1500 for more information.
